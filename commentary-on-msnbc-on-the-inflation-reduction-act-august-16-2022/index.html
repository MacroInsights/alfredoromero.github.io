Commentary on MSNBC on The Inflation Reduction Act August 16, 2022

February 3, 2023February 3, 2023 El Doc Romero

“Inflation is going to affect everybody, but particularly is going to affect people that have relatively lower budgets for energy, for food, for transportation because those people have a lower ability to save and a lower ability to cushion an increase in prices,” Dr. Alfredo Romero, an economics professor at North Carolina A&T, said on August 16, 2022 in an interview with José Díaz-Balart.
