Featured Initiative

Aggie Economic Index

The Aggie Economic Index (AEI) is a proprietary economic indicator developed at North Carolina A&T State University that tracks and forecasts regional economic conditions across North Carolina. The index synthesizes labor market data, consumer spending patterns, and business activity to provide timely, actionable economic intelligence for policymakers, businesses, and community leaders.
